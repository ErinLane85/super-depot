How it Works

Serious contacts only on pain of feather torture

  1. Always use encryption 256-bit minimum
  2. Payments accepted only in crypto with the exception of bitcoin. We do not accept bitcoin. Bitcoin has been compromised by Heroes, although the American Government still can't track it.
  3. Pick your villain for your job. Please note: if the villain doesn't like the job, you might be chosen as their next object lesson. You do not want to be their object lesson.
  4. All sales are final. Becoming the object lesson only means you get the honor of paying twice instead of once. Don't worry, your currency will be taken before your...lesson is administered. Enjoy.
